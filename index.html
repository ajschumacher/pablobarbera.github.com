About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Politics at New York University. My primary areas of research include social media and politics, electoral institutions and behavior, and political corruption.

I received a BA and MA in Political Science and Public Administration at the Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona. I also have a Master of Research in Political Science at the European University Institute, in Florence.
